Welcome to digiplasty

June 1, 2025

Hello there!

digiplasty is a collection some of my professional and academic projects and a place for research posts. It’s primarily about architecture, digital fabrication, computer graphics, AI, and software product management.

The word “digiplasty” originated from a digital sculpture collaboration, documented here. It captured a sense of digital materiality that, along with this domain name, has endured.

I also hope digiplasty becomes a catalyst for future collaborations.

If you would like to get in touch, find me on LinkedIn, or email me: contact [at] digiplasty [dot] com.
