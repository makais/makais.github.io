Welcome to digiplasty

Hello there! digiplasty is a collection some of my professional and academic projects and a place for research posts. It’s primarily about architecture, digital fabrication, computer graphics, AI, and software product management. The word “digiplasty” originated from a digital sculpture collaboration, documented here. It captured a sense of digital materiality that, along with this domain name, has endured.
